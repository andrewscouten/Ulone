# Akaza Shuki

## Deceased

Max 19 years old

The spitting image of a noble’s son. He is frail looking, slightly pale, and is dressed in fine silks. His blonde hair runs down to his cheeks and his green eyes are those of unburdened ones. 

He is the Tekina of Ryu, warden of fire, but met his end from a second Aikon of fire at Ninnan gate.
